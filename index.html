FUND OF KNOWLEDGE

Division of Cancer Medicine
Hematology/Oncology Fellowship Program
2021 Fund of Knowledge Examination

To better evaluate the success of our educational program and your progress during training, all fellows will complete this examination which will endeavor to determine your current fund of knowledge. If there are general deficiencies in a particular area, we hope to furnish you with a study guide, based on your answers, to assist in your progress towards the boards. If there are deficiencies among a majority of the fellows, the curriculum will be re-evaluated by the Fellowship Executive Committee.

Turn off all communication devices!!

You are on the honor system. No other browsers are to be launched while the exam is active. This includes any communication device.

There are eighty (80) questions in the original Fund of Knowledge exam and sixty (60) questions in the Hematology Fund of Knowledge exam. You will have two (2) hours and forty (40) minutes to complete the original examination and two (2) hours to finish the Hematology one. Your clock is in the upper left-hand corner of the screen under the title. You can go back to review questions and change answers. However, once you hit the submit button, your access to the examination has ended. A verification form has been provided. The last screen will display your test results. Record those results, sign and date the form and give it to the test proctor as you exit.
